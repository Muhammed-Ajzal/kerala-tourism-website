Thrissur Zoo

Thrissur Zoo located at distance of 2 km spreads over an area of thirteen acres. This is an ideal haunt for animal lovers. One can see tigers, camels, sweet mithun hippos, Pink flamingos, lion tailed macaque, sloth bear, and so many species of birds. The snake park has an impressive collection of reptiles. The endangered species like Asiatic Lion is found in Thrissur zoo. Inside the Zoo is the Natural History Museum.

Silent Valley National Parkr

Silent Valley, the evergreen national Park in Palakkad is a unique attraction of this place. The Silent Valley National park spreads an area of 90sq. km is situated in the north eastern corner of the district. The rarest species of animals and birds are seen in the valley. Apart from these the National Park houses more than thousand kinds of trees. The valley has a fair representation of all peninsular mammals. Visiting this National Park in Palakkad will be an exciting experience.
